COOK EASY

UI/UX, Branding | Recipe Application | Adobe Xd, Photoshop | Fall 2019

Industry

  • Home

Role

Solo Project

Duration

1 Week

Objective

The project aims at making a user-friendly helping the users choose what food they want to cook or eat. It also aims at merging culture with cooking by making users explore new type of cuisines they have never tried cooking.

The goal of this platform is:
1) To help users choose what kind of food they want to cook.
2) Let users try cooking exotic cuisines.

Summary

Cook Easy is a user oriented recipe application. When the user doesn’t know what he/she wants to cook, one can narrow down the options. Users can also search recipes by the ingredients they already have. Users can also add, rate, and share recipes.

If the user types the ingredients in the search bar, it automatically filters the recipes. The users can filter the recipes by less ingredients possible, most rated recipes including the ingredients, or the accuracy of the search.  As in accuracy, it means that including most ingredients possible from what the user typed in the search bar.

Along with that, there are step by step walkthroughs with words, and video tutorial walk-through so that even users with less experience in cooking can also enjoy cooking.

Even for users with a lot of cooking experiences, they can benefit from “Cook Easy” in that the recipes are catetorized by style, and ingredients.

Also, the user can make bookmarks of the recipes they want to keep. So if the user likes a certain recipe, or want to try it out next time he or she can find it easily from the bookmark tab. Users can also add, rate, and share recipes.

Problem Statement

Is there a way to merge culture with cooking?

Brainstorming

I started brainstorming by creating a mind map with the keyword "cooking"


Market Research

After brainstorming and researching existing products and services on the market from video tutorials like tasty, recipe applications like "Fork Over Knives" or "BBC Good Food", to even internet recipes, I soon realized that there was an opportunity to create a recipe product that would focus more on before the cooking experience.

Within the context of cooking, I decided to narrow down my focus to "knowing what to cook" before any action comes in. After narrowing my focus down to "pre-cooking", I soon realized that I also had a possibility in developing this application as a recipe app as well, with letting people try cooking exotic cuisines they have never tried cooking, or even eating.

Brand Identity

- The brand should be inclusive and universal.
- The design needs to be clean and simple.
- It should have an identity that works for all age groups.

Success Metrics

-The number of rates in recipes.
-The number of recipes in each categories.
-The diversity of cuisines.
-The accuracy of the search bar.
-The number of users using the app and the diversity of those users.
-The time spent on the application per individual user.

User Segments

-People with limited cooking experiences.
-People who don't know what they can cook with ingredients they already have.
-People who wants to try cooking a completely different cuisine.
-People who wants to explore all sorts of cuisine.

Cook Easy is the solution I brought up.

Reflection

During the one week of researching, designing, and prototyping, I learned to make rapid decisions and development. Moreover, I learned that the design have to fully reflect what the brand is aiming for, from colors to even types.

Due to time constraint, I could not focus on a lot of functions of the application. While I put much focus on finding recipes by the ingredients the user already has, I put less effort on the recipe itself. This way an important part of the application; the recipes has to be easily followable.

Next Steps

-Further develop the recipes so that the guides are easier to follow for first time cooking users.
-Do more studies for the style guide so that it portrays the brand identity.
-Conduct interviews and user testing.
